## Summary

At last the truth comes to light! As the crew learns of New Providence’ origins, Hildegarde’s dream, and Hildegarde’s fate, Tristan von Volsung makes his grand return to piracy, and the galactic balance of power will never be the same. Yet these fractures are not only political. The truth will test the loyalties of the Swallow’s crew as each decides where they stand, and the future they want to see.

And with Alan’s investigation at the heart of it, Alan must finally answer the questions he’s worked so hard to avoid. Who should control Gate travel? Where do his loyalties lie? How can he reconcile his pyrean and human heritage? And, most importantly, do he and Sven have a future together?

All dreams must end, and as the threads of the past fray and snap, Alan and the Swallow’s crew will chart their own future — if they can survive the present!

Starfarer's Dream is the fifth and final book in the Triple Strike series, and it includes seven illustrations plus additional bonus content.
